Membership Tiers

Dreamer Airlines offers three tiers of membership, each with increasing benefits:

  • Silver Member

    • Priority check-in and boarding
    • Earn miles on every flight
    • Exclusive discounts on baggage fees

  • Gold Member

    • All Silver Member benefits
    • Free seat selection on booking
    • Lounge access at select airports
    • Bonus miles on every flight

  • Platinum Member

    • All Gold Member benefits
    • Priority baggage handling
    • Complimentary upgrades (subject to availability)
    • Double miles on every flight
    • Dedicated customer support line

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the eligibility criteria for joining the Privileged Club?

    Anyone can join the Dreamer Airlines Privileged Club for free. There are no age or travel requirements.

  • How do I earn miles?

    You earn miles on every flight you take with Dreamer Airlines. The number of miles earned depends on your membership tier and the distance flown.

  • How can I redeem my miles?

    You can redeem your miles for a variety of rewards, including free flights, seat upgrades, and excess baggage allowance. Visit our website for more information.
